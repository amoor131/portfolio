More about me

When I'm not coding, I like to spend my time watching movies, playing videogames, listening to music and podcasts, painting, and learning.

My top three movies are currently Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind, Grand Budapest Hotel, and Raid 2. I wouldn't blame anyone for not liking any of these but I think they are each so entertaining and stylistic. I think they are best watched going in cold so I dont recommend trailers!

My go-to shows to put on in the background are It's Always Sunny In Philadelphia and New Girl. I've seen both of these shows at least 8 times through which means I can just listen to them while cleaning. If you haven't seen New Girl you're missing out, I assumed I wouldn't like it from the title but it's a remarkably funny show.

Video games are a guilty pleasure of mine. I've been trying to prioritize creative hobbies over consumption based ones like watching shows or playing games, but when I do choose to play them I gravitate towards games like no-build Fortnite, Valorant, and Clash of Clans
